Bishop Clement Simba

Bishop

Bishop Clement Simba has been serving Pentecostal Assemblies of God Machanga since 1997. With a deep passion for pastoral care and community outreach, he has grown the church into a spiritual home for many.

"My vision is to see the church grow in faith, love, and service to the community."

About Us

PAG Machanga is a Pentecostal church located in Embu County, Mbeere South. We are committed to spiritual growth, community service, and spreading the love of Christ.
